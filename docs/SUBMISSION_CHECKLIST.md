- [ ] Run one current-student travel/OPT case on the deployed site.

## Video

- [ ] Record a clear product demo with spoken audio.

- [ ] Keep the final video under three minutes.
- [ ] Use no copyrighted music or third-party footage.
- [ ] Upload the video to YouTube as **Public**.
- [ ] Open the YouTube link in a private window and verify playback and sound.
